My approach

After i got this project to redesign Odeon cinema for a young population, I started researching and exploring the famous cinema websites. I compared them to Odeon wesite and started getting idea of simplifying all of the process from login to booking a tickets.

Since the target was the young population I chose a vibrant palette to make it more eye catchy.
